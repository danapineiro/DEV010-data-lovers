Welcome to Mystic Tarot

Tarot is a set of cards used for divination and spiritual reflection. Each tarot card has unique symbolism and is believed to reveal information about a person's past, present, and future, as well as provide guidance and insights into their life. A tarot deck typically consists of 78 cards, divided into two main groups: the Major Arcana and the Minor Arcana. The Major Arcana comprises 22 cards that represent universal archetypes and concepts, such as The Fool, The Magician, The Empress, and so on. The Minor Arcana consists of 56 cards divided into four suits, similar to a traditional playing card deck, and each suit represents a specific facet of life, such as love, work, emotions, and challenges.
